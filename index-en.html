History

In 2004 I started at this company as an administrative assistant within the site in Roosendaal.
After various reorganisations, my location moved to Breda and I became an accounting assistant for the West Netherlands region. In addition, I designed some monitor tools such as a purchasing monitor and worked as complaints coordinator.
Welzorg went through a new major reorganization in 2016 and my position changed into Knowledge Management employee. With which I retained some tasks from my old role.
We then fully set up the knowledge base into a properly functioning system with various search methods and tools. This knowledge base is used by the entire customer contact center.
I have held my current position since 2018 and the knowledge base is a secondary task.

From 2000 to 2003 I worked as a Customer Service Representative at the telecom company T-mobile, previously Ben.
Within this company I have worked in various departments, including prepaid, postpaid, repairs and collection.
