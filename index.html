Hi and Welcome!

One thing in life that makes me happy is getting knowledge. It feeds me in a way. Don't ask me why.
That's why I took the course the Complete Web Development Bootcamp on Udemy. But however it's fun to get knowledge this way. Giving knowledge is an even better way to get knowledge.
My motto is giving knowledge = getting knowledge.
With this motto I try to share acquired knowledge and gather new knowledge to continue growth for organisations, coworkers and myself.

Carreer

In 2004 I started at Welzorg Nederland B.V. as an administrative assistant within the site in Roosendaal. After various reorganisations, my location moved to Breda and I became an accounting assistant for the West Netherlands region. In addition, I designed some monitor tools such as a purchasing monitor and worked as complaints coordinator. Welzorg went through a new major reorganization in 2016 and my position changed into Knowledge Management employee. With which I retained some tasks from my old role. We then fully set up the knowledge base into a properly functioning system with various search methods and tools. This knowledge base was used by the entire customer contact center. I have held my current position since 2018 and the knowledge base is a secondary task.
From 2000 to 2003 I worked as a Customer Service Representative at the telecom company T-mobile, previously Ben. Within this company I have worked in various departments, including prepaid, postpaid, repairs and collection.
Look at my LinkedIn profile for more information about my carreer.
